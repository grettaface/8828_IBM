Applications without borders

Software-defined data centers (SDDCs) are virtualizing all the elements of traditional IT infrastructure and delivering them as a service.

SDDCs provide:

Seamless cloud infrastructures with software-defined network and storage.
Frictionless, intuitive experiences that let you choose where you want your applications to be.
Flexible workload capacity that scales to meet your specific needs.
